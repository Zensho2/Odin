Tuna for your car

Ingredients


  • 1 can of tuna (the kind you have to guard with your life from your cat's relentless pursuit)
  • 1 luxurious bowl (because nothing but the best will do)
  • A dash of love (optional, but highly recommended)

    • Instructions


  • 1- Begin by delicately opening the can of tuna, taking care to do this in a different room, so your cat doesn’t realize you’re preparing their next meal.

  • 2- Drain the tuna, while your cat watches with laser-focused eyes, convinced you’re about to make them the meal of a lifetime.

  • 3- Flake the tuna into the bowl, arranging it with the care of a fine sushi chef. Remember, presentation is key—even if your cat will devour it in 3 seconds flat.

  • 4- Serve the tuna with a flourish, placing the bowl in your cat’s favorite dining spot, and step back to admire your culinary masterpiece.

  • 5- Watch as your cat devours the tuna, then demands more, reminding you that this is their world, and you’re just living in it..

  • Bon appétit!
